About Me

Hello, I'm Joyti Jadwani

I'm a professional mehndi artist based in Nohar with over 5 years of experience in creating stunning henna designs for all occasions. My journey in mehndi art began as a passion and has now become my profession.

Specializing in bridal mehndi, I also create beautiful designs for festivals, weddings, and special events. My unique style blends traditional patterns with contemporary elements to create designs that are both timeless and trendy.
